Wusang: Isle of Blaq

World's Ultra Simple Asset Numbers Game

Wusang: Isle of Blaq is a very simple game of chance.

It is a game that anyone can easily play and earn tokens on the Hive blockchain. The goal of Wusang is to have fun by earning Hivebits (HBIT) and, maybe, by finding other treasure tokens hidden around the Isle of Blaq.

Wusang?

Wusang is an acronym for: World's Ultra Simple Asset Numbers Game

  • World's - anyone in the world can play it
  • Ultra - extremely
  • Simple - not just simple, ultra simple
  • Asset - you get crypto assets
  • Numbers - there is a random element involved
  • Game - you get HBIT each time, but if you're lucky, you may get more

Playing Wusang: Isle of Blaq is ultra simple: get a Hive account and "mine" for treasure using an HBIT or Wusang command (like !HBIT or !wusang) in reply to one of your own posts or comments on Hive.
