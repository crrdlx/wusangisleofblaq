Wusang: Isle of Blaq

World's Ultra Simple Asset Numbers Game

Wusang: Isle of Blaq is a very simple game of chance.

It is a game that anyone can play and earn tokens on the Hive blockchain. The goal of Wusang is fun by earning HBIT and, maybe, finding other treasures.

The idea for Wusang

The LUV token was created to share love with others on the Hive chain, then Hivebits (HBIT) spun off as a way for Hive users to earn for themselves due to their own effort. Hivebits has been about your effort earning you a reward. And, Hivebits will stay that way...your effort will get you one HBIT each day as your "pay" for your work. Yet, from the start of HBIT, there was a desire to eventually include some type of randomness. Like rolling dice at the casino table, randomness adds an element of fun. So, now HBIT integrate into Wusang and will mix the consistent and stable element of your-effort-means-your-reward every time, along with a random chance of possibly getting another, extra treasure token. You might not get another token, then again...there's a chance!
