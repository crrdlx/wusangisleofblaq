The story of Wusang: Isle of Blaq

World's Ultra Simple Asset Numbers Game

Somewhere in the exotic Wusang region of the tropics, lies the mysterious Isle of Blaq.

The isle itself is shrouded in mystery. It's location is unknown. There are even rumors that it is a floating island...it's location always changing. There are rumors of ghosts haunting the isle. There are tales of piracy...pirates of old and modern day pirates. Some say the pearls drive men to madness.

Treasures untold.

Only one thing about the Isle of Blaq is certain: it holds a wealth of treasure. It is world reknown for its beautiful pearls...the famed pearls of Wusang. The pearls drew treasure seekers to the waters of Wusang. And that drew pirates. And that resulted in jewels, precious metals, and battles for them all. Treasures were lost and lie on the sea floor. And there are treasures that have never even been found. These treasures are there, laying in wait, ready to be mined and harvested by the first comers to dive and mine the riches of Wusang: Isle of Blaq.
