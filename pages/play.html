Play Wusang: Isle of Blaq

World's Ultra Simple Asset Numbers Game

How to play

Playing Wusang: Isle of Blaq is simple. You "mine" for treasure using a Wusang command.

  1. You need a Hive blockchain account. If you do not have one, you can get a free account here or learn about Hive and see other options on WhyHive.co
  2. You need to comment/reply and include one of the Wusang commands, such as !WUSANG in that reply post.
  3. Your comment/reply must be in response to one of your own posts or comments, else it won't work. This rule is to eliminate possible spam posts, replies, and notifications from flooding others' accounts.
  4. You can play once per day. No need to try more than once per day, it will not go through.

Good luck and happy mining!

How it works

Here's what happens when you "mine" for treasure with a Wusang command like !WUSANG.

  1. The Wusang bot will pick up your command and issue one HBIT to you. Consider this your daily "pay" for your effort in mining.
  2. The bot will also generate a random number. This number will determine if you find an extra treasure, like a BLAQ pearl.
  3. It's possible to monitor your random number, and token transfers, in the log channel found in the LUV-HBIT Discord at https://discord.gg/K5GvNhcPqR.
  4. In a short time, the Wusang bot should send you a confirmation reply to your comment containing the Wusang command you typed. It's usually around a minute, but sometimes there are delays.
  5. You can check your transfer on a Hive-Engine explorer like https://he.dtools.dev and entering your username. Or, you can see your holdings in your wallet, such as at https://tribaldex.com/wallet.
